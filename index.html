work process

01

Briefing

The start of work begins after receiving the completed brief and all the necessary materials for the project. The stage includes a discussion of the project objectives, ways of their implementation, as well as technical issues.

02

Analytics

Conducting a competitive analysis, researching the target audience, their pains and needs. Creating a model of target user behavior on the site. Analytics in the development of sites makes it possible to identify the main preferences of users, on the basis of which it is possible to develop a site that contributes to the development of the company and attracting new customers.

03

Prototyping

Schematic creation of the project architecture. At the prototyping stage, not only the functionality of the project is laid, but also usability, site structure and nuances of user behavior are thought out. The main goal of prototyping is to test how consistent the user's journey is and to identify obstacles that may arise in the course of their interaction with the product.

04

concept

Visual design of layouts in accordance with prototypes and terms of reference. The stage includes adding photos, graphic elements, searching for interesting compositional solutions, working with references, selecting fonts and styles.

05

Design

Rendering of all pages in a given style, adaptive versions design.

06

Final

The project is ready for development. As a result, you will receive a figma file, including sources and adaptive.
